Night at Boomer's

This game is a parody of Scott Cawthon's Five Nights at Freddy's.

Objective: Protect yourself from Boomer the eagle while you work in a very familiar looking building after hours. Your night shift lasts from 12AM to 6AM.

 

This game has flashing lights and loud sounds!

 

Play the game:

WebGL (play in your web browser!)

Android APK (play on your phone!)
